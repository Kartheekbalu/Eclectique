2. Spark Tech

Venue: Academic Block-1

Participation Fee: 60/-

Spark Tech, a super exciting technical quiz that tests participants' academic knowledge.

Structure of the Event:

  • 🔋 Round 1: Participants will group up and take a quiz.
  • 🔋 Round 2: Chosen teams will encounter a blank-filling test.
  • 🔋 Round 3: A few chosen groups will receive questions with multiple options.
  • 🔋 Round 4: The best groups from previous rounds will participate in a rapid-fire round.

😎 Eligibility: All engineering students, no matter their year, are welcome!

🎓 Certification: All participants will receive a participation certificate!

Join Us and follow these instructions with extreme excitement!

  • 🤝 All questions are based on EEE department subjects only.
  • 🤝 Groups will be formed by the event coordinator.
  • 🤝 Formation of groups by yourself will be unacceptable.
